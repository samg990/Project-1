Our Mission

We have the sole mission of reaching as many youth as possible and having a positive impact on their lives.

How?

By providing willing donors direct access to mentors, we are promoting this goal. Mentors work hands on with youth and play an invaluable role in molding them into productive, successful, florishing adults.

Where?

Currently, FMLY works with Los Angeles non-profits. The goal is to spread nation-wide within the next two years!

Interested in Getting Involved?

The Process is simple...

  • DONOR
  • MENTOR

DONOR

Simply, Log in, by clicking the Donate button on the Enter Page.

From there you have access to every registered mentor and the activites with their mentees that they want you to sponsor.

Choose an activity. Donate. And, just like that, you've had a direct impact on someone's life!

MENTOR

Connect with your local Non-profit. They will go through their standard registration process, provide you with training, and inform you of their mentor guidelines, standards, and expections.

Once you're registered with them, you can sign up on our website, listing them as your organization. They will confirm.

From there, you simply Post activities that you want to do with your mentee... Now get to changing the world! People want to help you. We promise!
